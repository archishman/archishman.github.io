Canary

Canary is a mobile application that makes social listening a more enjoyable enjoyable experience. Our application integrates with popular streaming services and allows users to build social music profiles and build shared listening queues. Our tech stack includes Postgres, Redis, koa.js, Swift/UIKit. Learn more about Canary on our website.

Boa

A low-cost smartphone based retinal scan to diagnose AD. To diagnose a patient, a visoScope lens must be clipped onto the smartphone and the subject’s retina must be imaged via the app. This image is then sent to a server and a diagnosis is returned within minutes.

Manush

Manush is a chess engine that uses a combination of reinforcement learning and supervised learning to play human-like, low-level chess built similarly to Google DeepMind's AlphaZero enigne. The objective isn't to produce a strong chess player, but rather to produce a realistic sparring partner for amateur chess players. The project is still in progress. You can see more on the repository.

Research in Combinatorial Game Theory

Combinatorial games are a special type of perfect information games. A comply/constrain game or a game with a Muller twist is a combinatorial game where players are allowed to place constraints on their opponent's moves. In this paper, we explore the periodicity of Grundy values of a special type of such games.
